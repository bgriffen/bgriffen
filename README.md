### Hi, I’m Brendan.

I’m currently Chief Technology Officer and co-founder at [Dynomics Inc.](https://www.dynomics.com), a venture backed startup focused on reversing heart failure, deploying complex *in silico* and *in vitro* systems at the intersection of bioengineering, computer science, robotics, tissue engineering and cell biology.
